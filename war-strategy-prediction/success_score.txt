Success Score = 
  Objective Achievement (0-50 points) +
  Efficiency (0-30 points) + 
  Speed (0-10 points) +
  Strategic Impact (0-10 points)


1. Objective Achievement (0-50 pts)
if objective_fully_achieved: 50 points
if objective_partially_achieved: 25 points  
if objective_failed: 0 points
if exceeded_objectives: +10 bonus

2. Efficiency/Cost Effectiveness (0-30 pts)
Based on Casualty Rate = (Casualties / Total Troops) × 100
< 1% casualty rate: 30 points
1-5% casualty rate: 25 points
5-10% casualty rate: 20 points
10-20% casualty rate: 15 points
20-30% casualty rate: 10 points
> 30% casualty rate: 5 points


3. Speed/Duration (0-10 pts)
Completed in 1 day or less: 10 points
2-3 days: 8 points
4-7 days: 6 points
8-14 days: 4 points
15+ days: 2 points


4. Strategic Impact (0-10 pts)
Decisive strategic victory: 10 points
Major tactical advantage: 8 points
Moderate gains: 6 points
Minimal impact: 4 points
No strategic value: 2 points


🎖️ Military Success Categories:
90-100: Exemplary Victory
80-89: Decisive Success  
70-79: Solid Victory
60-69: Acceptable Outcome
50-59: Pyrrhic Victory
<50:  Costly/Unsuccessful
